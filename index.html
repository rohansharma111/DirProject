Biography

Director Ramesh Sharma, who is connected to B.R. Films, had worked as an assistant director under B.R. Chopra and Ravi Chopra for decades. Born in Uttar Pradesh, Director Ramesh Sharma has built an impressive career spanning films, television serials, and advertisements.
